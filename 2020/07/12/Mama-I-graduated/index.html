Mama I graduated!

Tanner Phan
12-Jul-2020

Even though I had already started grad school, I hope it’s not too late to thank Seattle Pacific University, the ECS Department, and the professors I was fortunate to have had. The university was kind enough to enroll me in the last minute notice. It’s unfortunate that Professors are not paid more for the kind of tuition we were paying. I’m currently in business school, and they would say that something is wrong with this business model. I’ve told my Profs this: I think professors should be treated like rockstars. Maybe when AI works better, and then we can build an auto-grader for every subject so that professors can stop grading and feeling bad for felling students and killing their dreams.

I had a chance to meet and work with a group of really compassionate students at SPU. As we learned, society is a messy place. I pitched the idea of university education because it was manageable. I’m thankful the group agreed because I learned a ton from doing the research and working ya’ll. We spent Winter Quarter interviewing people and sorting through our research data, and then we came up with a theme to focus on.

In Spring, the quarter that followed, even though COVID19 happened, and went remote learning, our group still met weekly on Zoom, and eventually came up with a draft for an app that solves some of the problems we saw in our research. Our research from Seattle Pacific University can be found is here: http://bubulapp.com

Now, I’m excited to be accepted to UW Foster for the MSIS program. I wasn’t sure if this is really was going to happen. Now three weeks in, I guess it is. In my current free time, I’m working on Studimore. It’s an app that aims to make college more fun. Honestly, it’s something I wish I had when I was struggling starting out in my undergrad Coming in September!
